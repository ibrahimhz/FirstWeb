Our Story

We wanted to share with the world some beautiful sites in Syria. The media tends to ignore the beautiful and historical sites that it has. Some, including myself, would even describe it as heaven on Earth before the ongoing conflict started in the region. Unfortunately, some locations have been completely destroyed and I wanted to show the world what those places looked like before. Many of these locations have been renovated and there's ongoing construction and efforts to try and rebuild an even better and more beautiful Syria.
